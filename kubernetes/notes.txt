[ Concepts ]

- creates a desired state in the cluster

Deployment
- the desired state

- pods can be scaled

Pod
- runs 1 container or containers of different types

Service
- describes how the communication between
Pods - pod to pod
Cluster IP - to load balanced pods
NodePort - External world to deployment
